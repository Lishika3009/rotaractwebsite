CLUB SERVICES

The Club Services Committee is an essential component of a Rotaract Club, dedicated to ensuring the smooth functioning and overall development of the club itself. This committee plays a vital role in organizing internal activities and managing the club's infrastructure.The Club Services Committee focuses on attracting and retaining members, ensuring their engagement and satisfaction within the club.

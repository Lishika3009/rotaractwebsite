SELF DEFENCE WORKSHOP

The Rotaract Club of Thapar University recently conducted a self- defence workshop for boys and girls of class 7th and 8th at Playways public Senior Secondary School.
The worshop was aimed at teaching self defence techniques to help the students to protect themselves in potentially dangerous situations. Techniques like blocking, striking and grappling were taught in the workshop
The workshop was well recieved by the students who actively partcipated in the training sessions and showed enthusiasm to learn more. Moreover this helped them build up confidence at times of difficulty.

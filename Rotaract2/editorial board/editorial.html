READATHON AND TEACHERS DAY

Nava Nalanda Central Library organised Punjab's first ever READATHON which lasted for over 24 hours. Rotract along with other socities of TIET actively helped in the smooth conduction of this exciting event.
Rotaract club of TIET also celebrated teachers day. Gratitude was expressed to them to with handcrafts as our expression of gratitude for their hard work and dedication to our society's future generation.
